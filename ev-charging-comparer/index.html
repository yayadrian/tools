EV charging planner

Compare chargers by price, pace, and real car limits.

Set your vehicle once, then stack up home, destination, and rapid chargers side by side. Charge times use the lower of the charger speed and your car's own AC or DC limit, so the table reflects what you can actually get.

Initial setup

Add your car name and charging caps. Costs default to pound sterling, but the symbol is editable.

Assumptions

This keeps the comparison simple and transparent.

Energy to add is entered as kWh. Time is calculated as energy ÷ effective kW. Total cost is price per kWh × energy added.

If a charger is faster than your car can accept, the comparison automatically caps the session at the car's AC or DC maximum.

Charging options

Add as many chargers as you want. The comparison table updates instantly.

Comparison table

Click any column heading to sort.

Charge time is based on the effective speed after applying your vehicle limit.

Tip: if you're comparing the same top-up amount across chargers, sorting by total cost shows the cheapest option, while sorting by charge time shows the quickest.
